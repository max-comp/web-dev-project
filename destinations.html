Featured Destination of the Month

Santorini, Greece

Discover the magic of Santorini, where white-washed buildings cascade down volcanic cliffs and stunning sunsets paint the sky in brilliant hues of orange and pink.

Highlights

  • Watch the famous Oia sunset
  • Explore ancient Akrotiri ruins
  • Visit black sand beaches
  • Take a caldera cruise

Travel Information

  • Best Time to Visit: June to September
  • Average Temperature: 23°C (73°F)
  • Local Currency: Euro (€)
  • Language: Greek
Quick Tips
  • ✓ Book accommodations in advance
  • ✓ Rent a car for island exploration
  • ✓ Bring comfortable walking shoes
